Explore Ancient Egypt

Egypt is a land of mystery and wonder, where you can see some of the oldest monuments in human history. It is a perfect destination for those who love history and sunshine.

Historic Sites

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the only one of the seven wonders of the ancient world that is still standing today.

  • The Great Sphinx
  • Luxor Temple
  • The Nile River

Fun Activities

Beyond the history, you can enjoy many modern activities like desert safaris or world-class diving in the Red Sea.

  1. Take a camel ride near the pyramids
  2. Visit the Egyptian Museum in Cairo
  3. Go snorkeling in Sharm El-Sheikh
